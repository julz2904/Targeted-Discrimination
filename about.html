About Targeted Discrimination

Targeted discrimination is a type of discrimination that aims towards a group of individuals of a specific race, background, culture, etc. Targeted discrimination is used to harass and discriminate a group based off of their identity, such as racism towards people of color and immigrants, but also discriminating against people who are transgender (sexism and transphobia), people who are practicing their religion (catholicism, christianity, buddhism, etc.), and many, many other types of discriminatory behavior.
